Recent Project:

Paul Grass website redesign


Paul Grass had an unwieldy WordPress site needing to be fixed.
We redesigned for easy management and an improved user experience.

Creative services

  • Web design and development
  • Information architecture
  • User experience


Paul Grass is a classical art illustrator and painter. He depends on marketing his work for commissions and enrolling students. His site was difficult to manage and he needed a more useable and attractive site to engage clients.

I worked with Paul in tandem with a class assignment which focused on building site requirements. Through the discovery process we identified core functions. A newsletter signup, interactive class calendar, and lightbox gallery were developed. Since the redesign the site receives more visits and Paul's updates are easier to perform.
