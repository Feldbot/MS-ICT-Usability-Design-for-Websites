Recent Print Project:

Journal American Water Works Association


Journal - AWWA is the world's premier water industry periodical.
It has been in production for more than a century.

Creative services

  • Art direction
  • Graphic design
  • Illustration
  • Production


I fulfill art direction and graphic design duties for Journal - AWWA. I illustrate the cover and article concepts, organize the layouts, and oversee the production of the complex technical art. I'm honored to use my skills to inspire dedicated water industry employees.

The cover concept examines flags and global boundaries, while the countries represented are AWWA's international business partners. Below are design highlights including a feature about the AWWA India initiative, and a "Spotlight On" article relaying the historic and architectural value of American water utilities.
